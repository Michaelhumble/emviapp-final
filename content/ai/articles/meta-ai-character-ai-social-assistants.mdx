# Meta AI & Character.AI: Social Assistants, Done Right

Use AI where you already spend time—WhatsApp, Instagram, mobile. Here’s how to keep it useful and safe.

## TL;DR
- Meta AI: great for quick prompts in WhatsApp/Instagram; check your region/country availability.
- Character.AI: creative roleplay/brainstorming; set guardrails; customize bots.
- Privacy: review data policies; opt‑out where available; avoid sharing sensitive data.

## Where they shine
- Meta AI: travel ideas, quick summaries, image generation (varies by rollout); seamless in chat apps.
- Character.AI: ideation, dialog writing, “brainstorm buddy,” and creative scenes; massive engagement.

## Setup & safety
1) Update apps (WhatsApp/Instagram); look for Meta AI in search or chat.
2) Character.AI: create an account; explore top bots; set your bot’s boundaries.
3) Review policies; opt‑out or limit data sharing if offered; keep it light.

## Real examples
- WhatsApp: “Suggest 3 weekend trips within 3 hours of [city] with budget and links.”
- Instagram: “Write 3 captions with relevant hashtags for this product photo.”
- Character.AI: “Roleplay as a UX mentor. Ask questions then critique my portfolio.”

## Try it now
Prompts to paste:
- Meta AI: “Turn this photo into a clean, minimalist banner. Add subtle gradient and crisp typography.”
- Character.AI: “You’re an interviewer. Ask 5 progressively deeper questions about my startup idea.”

## FAQ
1) Is Meta AI available in my country? Rollouts expand; see latest announcements.
2) Is Character.AI safe for kids? Supervise and discuss boundaries.

## Key takeaways
- Social assistants are convenient; get value but protect your privacy.
