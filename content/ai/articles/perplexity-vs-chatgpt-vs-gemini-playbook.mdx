# The Ultimate Perplexity vs ChatGPT vs Gemini Playbook

Pair the right tools at the right steps—research, outline, draft, verify—to move faster with fewer mistakes.

## TL;DR
- Research: Perplexity (citations) + Gemini (freshness)
- Draft: ChatGPT (structure) + Claude (tone)
- Verify: Perplexity + Gemini links

## The 4‑step stack
1) Research → Perplexity, Gemini
2) Outline → ChatGPT
3) Draft → ChatGPT/Claude
4) Verify → Perplexity/Gemini

## Prompts by stage
- Research: “Summarize last 30 days of changes in [topic]. Cite sources with dates.”
- Outline: “Create an H2/H3 outline with TL;DR and 2 tables. Add 10 FAQs.”
- Draft: “Write in a sharp, energetic voice. Mix short/long sentences. No AI clichés.”
- Verify: “List claims without links. Attach sources with dates. Flag weak evidence.”

## Example projects
- Product launch brief → pair Perplexity/Gemini → draft in ChatGPT → polish in Claude.
- SEO article → outline in ChatGPT → examples via Gemini → verify with Perplexity.

## FAQ
1) Why pair tools? Each excels at different steps; pairing reduces errors.
2) Can I do it all in one model? You can—quality is usually lower.

## Key takeaways
- Use a repeatable stack; ship faster with fewer mistakes.
